Welcome to Viva La Vida


British Rock band Coldplay's masterpiece of an album, Viva La Vida or Death and All His Friends was released on the 12th of June, 16 years ago. Since then, it has made its mark as one of the greatest albums of the 2000s

Today we'll be looking at everything that makes this album what it is, from the songs, to the places, to the people. Let's dive in!
